FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: WHAT IS BRANDED DROPSHIPPING?

Branded dropshipping is very similar to traditional dropshipping but gives the customer more confidence when purchasing, resulting in more conversions and profit. We teach you step-by-step how to successfully build and brand your store.

Q: HOW MUCH MONEY DO I NEED TO START?

This answer varies from person to person, but generally, we recommend you have anywhere from $200-$500 to completely set up your store and fund your ads.

Q: I HAVE ZERO KNOWLEDGE, CAN I STILL JOIN?

Yes, we’ve had plenty of members join who have zero experience with branded dropshipping and get profitable within their first month of being inside the community.

Q: CAN I JOIN FROM ANOTHER COUNTRY?

Yes, our course is designed for everyone regardless of where you’re located. We have countless members based all around the world who are killing it with e-commerce thanks to our community.

Q: DO YOU TEACH HOW TO RUN ADS?

Yes, we primarily teach you how to run our meta ad strategy. You have full in-depth videos on campaign setup along with live scaling videos of a store I am currently running.

Q: HOW LONG UNTIL I SEE PROFIT?

Generally speaking, most members see profit within their first month of being inside the community, but it comes down to the amount of work you put in. We can’t do it for you!
